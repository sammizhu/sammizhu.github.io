Tired of waking up tired?

Coded using Python Flask and JavaScript, Sleeps50 is a personalized dynamic web application where users can track their sleeping habits and dreams to improve their health, mood, and mentality. It multifunctions as an online diary where users can record their dreams and reflect on the day ahead before getting out of bed. With multiple features in addition to the diary log, users can also view their sleep habit analytics, which is beneficial for users who need to track their sleeping health for medical purposes. The web app also hosts resources that users can use to improve their sleep health if needed.
