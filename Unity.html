Explore Your Fantasy World

Immerse yourself in an enchanting forest, stumbled upon a world filled with new and exciting sights. Embrace your newfound freedom as you venture through the tranquil woods and explore the numerous plants that call it home. Designed to offer you moment of peace as you escape the chaos of everyday life, this game invites you to the unique opportunity to experience an adventure through a fantasy world as you seek for your rabbit.

Note: This is a game developed by Hewan Kidanemariam and myself.
